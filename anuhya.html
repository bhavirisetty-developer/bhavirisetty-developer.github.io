The Designer's Story

Hi, I'm Anuhya, and I am a fashion designer specializing in fabric painting. For more than 15 years, I have been pouring my creativity and passion into every brushstroke and design. While my degrees in Fashion Technology and an MBA gave me the strategic knowledge to build a business, my family life as a mother of two has been my greatest inspiration. My work is a reflection of this journey—a perfect blend of technical skill, business savvy, and a touch of heartfelt emotion. I create unique, custom-designed dresses for clients who appreciate artful craftsmanship and personal stories woven into their fashion. I believe every dress should tell a story, and I am committed to helping my clients wear a piece of their own narrative.

Services Offered

Brush & Bloom

Fabric painting transforms plain textiles into personalized works of art, adding vibrant patterns and unique designs to any piece. It's a creative and accessible way to express your style and give new life to your clothing or home décor. With fabric painting, you can make a wardrobe with custom colors by learning to apply your own beautiful, long-lasting designs. View Brochure

Soundarya Vastra

Transform fabrics with skilled craftsmanship, from meticulous saree finishing to tailored dress stitching. We specialize in intricate maggam work, a traditional embroidery technique using beads, sequins, and threads to create stunning, one-of-a-kind designs. These bespoke services ensure every garment fits perfectly and showcases exquisite, personalized detail. View Brochure

Telugu Palukulu

Join our comprehensive Telugu language program to master reading, writing, and speaking all the letters and fundamentals. This course empowers you to confidently interact with your elders, from parents to grandparents, and build deeper family connections. By learning Telugu, you will help keep our beautiful language and rich heritage alive for generations to come. View Brochure
